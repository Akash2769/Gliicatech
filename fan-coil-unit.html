FCU for Electric Enclosures

Protect your enclosures against heat, dust, and moisture for hassle-free performance and the safety of your sensitive electronics.

About Gliicatech FCU

Gliicatech introduces you with our newly designed FCU for electric enclosure. These Panel mounted FCU’s are designed to offer your enclosures protection against Heat, Dust & Moisture/Humidity ensuring that you experience hassle free performance from the machine & Making sure that vital, expensive and sensitive electronic components are always protected.

Key Features

  • Cooling Capacity: 1500 watt to 3500 watt.
  • Single Air Flow: single internal air circuit which circulates cool air In the panel.
  • Single Water Flow: chilled water is pumped in the machine which is used as coolant and the circuit dissipates extracted heat through the water outlet .
  • Fans: standard model fans, easily available spares.
  • Enclosure: Sleek design, helping enhance overall look of your machine/product. Fully ss_304 body-for pharma and food packaging industries. MS/GI powder coated body.
  • Temperature controller: temperature controller with LED/LCD display, with auto-cut and auto-start technology.

100% customizable designs available For replacement with old machine

Customizable Designs

In situations where chilled water is running in the system or there is very less space for mounting regular PAC,s. GLIICATECH WATER TO AIR HEAT EXCHANGERS can be used to transfer heat from the Panel to chilled water circulating in the coil.

There are no filters to be cleaned and no hot air is expelled In the environment.These systems are ideal for use in contaminated, Dusty environment. Here, in-house chilled water can be used or a Separate chiller can be used to suit different applications.

Applications

  • Medical Electronic Equipment/BFS Machines
  • Process Control Equipment
  • CPU/Server enclosures.
  • Robotics
  • Any panel hosing with sensitive electronic componets.
