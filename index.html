About Us

Gliicatech is a trusted leader in the manufacturing of high-performance panel air conditioners , FCU (fan coil unit ) for industrial and commercial use. In addition to our cutting-edge AC solutions, we offer comprehensive services including AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts), servicing, maintenance, and repairs for panel air conditioners, oil chillers, and water chillers. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer care ensures that your cooling systems run smoothly and efficiently, providing reliable performance year-round.

Our

Services

A/C Installation

Gliicatech offers installation as well as shifting services of residential, Commercial and industrial Air-Conditioners, using professional Equipment's with our well trained staff .

Maintenance & Repair

We provide a full range of repairing, maintenance and AMC(Annual maintenance contract ) of Panel-AC, Commercial AC, Industrial AC, Water chiller, Spindle chiller & all types of HVAC systems.

HVAC Spares

All types of HVAC spares are available at Gliicatech, including standard model fans, compressors, relays, motors, filters, capacitors, coils, thermostats, switches, sensors, and more.

Pre-Season Inspection

A thorough check of your HVAC system to spot and address potential issues early. Our experts inspect key components like filters, coils, and refrigerant levels to ensure smooth and efficient operation.

Annual Technical Inspections

Annual inspection of machines helps a technician to identify Dirty coils, any burned or miscellaneous /faults components / Burned contactors or relays before they cause any long term Damage to the system.

Air Quality Solutions

Gliicatech offers air quality monitoring and solutions, including air purifiers and humidity control systems, to ensure a clean and healthy indoor environment in both residential and commercial spaces.
