Panel Air Conditioners

Heavy-duty air conditioners for electric enclosures to protect against heat, dust, and moisture.

About Gliicatech Panel ACs

Gliicatech specializes in heavy-duty air conditioners designed to protect your enclosures from heat, dust, and humidity. Ensure long life and reliability for your critical electronic equipment with our innovative solutions.

  • Cooling Capacity: 250W to 2600W
  • Dual Air Circuit: The internal air circuit circulates cool air In the panel & external air circuit dissipates extracted heat To the atmosphere.
  • Compressor: Hermetic compressors with Reciprocatary/Rotary & Scroll technology.
  • Fans: Standard model fans, easily available spares.
  • Enclosure: Sleek design, helping enhance overall look of your machine/product. Fully ss_304 body, for pharma and food packaging industries.
  • Temperature controller: Temperature controller with LED/LCD display, with auto-cut and auto-start technology.
  • IP PROTECTION: Standard internal IP protection to the Enclosure.
  • Alarm: Where required, Sound alarm can be provided as per Demand for High Temp & Low Temp at no extra cost.
  • Drain Pipe: Drain pipe is provided to remove condensate/Moisture from the panel. Extra pipe is provided for extension.

CUSTOMIZABLE UNITS ARE ALSO AVAILABLE FOR REPLACEMENT WITH INPORTED UNITS.
